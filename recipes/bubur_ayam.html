Bubur Ayam

Bubur Ayam. With sambal and soy sauce as condiments

Bubur Ayam is another classic staples in Indo-Melayu dish , it literrally translates to chicken porridge. It is served warm, with condiments such as sambal, soy sauce (sweet/salty), and drizzled with kerupuk.

  1. The Ingredients
    • Rice
    • Water
    • Chicken
    • Garlic
    • Salt
    • Sugar
  2. Cooking Steps
    1. Wash your rice
    2. Submerge your washed rice inside a pan
    3. Let it slow cook until porridge texture achieved
    4. Cut up the chicken into thin slices, throw inside the porridge
    5. Let it simmer while you add salt and sugar
  3. Bubur Ayam is Served!
